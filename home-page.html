Did Halloween Come Early?

For too long the world has been overrun by clothing that simply should not be worn. enough is enough! What Not To Wear is here to lend voice to the majority who has been silent too long. The majority who has been forced to deal with the negative externality of ensembles that should not see the light of day.

And now, we're back to take on another abomination of apparal-nation... the scurge of pet costumes. Dogs may be a man's (or woman's. Hands up don't shoot 2017 PC police) best friend, but that doesn't mean they agreed to play dress up!

You know what they say the first step to causing change is to bring light to the problem. So join us as we put terrible trappings on blast one outfit at a time...
